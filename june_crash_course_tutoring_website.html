June Holiday Math Crash Course

Home-based small-group tuition in Punggol Central for Secondary 1–4 students taking E Mathematics or A Mathematics.

About the Crash Course

This June crash course is designed to help students strengthen weak topics, revise important concepts, and gain more confidence before school assessments. Lessons are kept small so that each student can receive closer guidance and ask questions more freely.

Subjects Offered

  • Secondary 1–4 E Mathematics
  • Secondary 3–4 A Mathematics
  • NA and Express students are welcome

Class Format

  • Home-based tuition at Punggol Central
  • Small group lessons
  • Minimum 2 students to start
  • Maximum 4 students per class

Teaching Focus

  • Step-by-step concept explanation
  • Topic-focused revision
  • Worksheets and guided practice
  • Mistake correction and exam techniques

Pricing

Secondary 1–2 (E Mathematics) $28/hour per student
Secondary 3–4 (E Mathematics / A Mathematics) $30/hour per student
Recommended lesson duration: 1.5 to 2 hours per session.
Final class timing will depend on students' availability and preferred topics.

Who This Is For

Students Who Need Revision

Suitable for students who want to revisit weaker topics and build stronger foundations during the June holidays.

Students Preparing for WA/EOY

Useful for students who want early preparation through guided practice, worksheets, and common question types.

Students Who Prefer Small Groups

Small groups allow students to learn with friends while still receiving close attention and support.
